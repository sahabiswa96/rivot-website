FAQs

What is the range of the nx100?
The nx100 starts with a base range of 80km, and this can be upgraded up to about 280–300 km (depending on variant and usage).
What is the average price of the NX100?
The nx100 is priced starting at around ₹ 89,000, with the average cost varying based on the chosen variant and features.
What are the variants available?
Variants include Classic, Pro, Max, Sports, and Offlander.
How large is the storage / boot space?
It offers a 45 litre boot that can accommodate two helmets and groceries.
Where is the NX100 manufactured?
The NX100 is produced at Rivot Motors’ first scooter manufacturing factory in Belagavi, Karnataka (India).
What unique features does the nx100 offer?
The NX100 comes packed with industry first and exciting features, including an APU extra battery for extended range, gesture-based boot opening, front and rear ride cameras, Boost Mode for instant power, and more premium innovations designed for safety, convenience, and thrill.
Are there any smart features in the nx100?
Along with power and range, the nx100 comes with connected features, smart navigation, and real time ride analytics, making it one of the most advanced scooters in its class.
