Code of Ethics

RIVOT MOTORS India Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 1956 and having its registered address at Fifth Floor, Oneness, 1st Cross, 1st Main, Sadashivnagar, Belagavi, Karnataka - 590001 (hereinafter referred to as “RIVOT®” or “We” or “Our” or “Us”) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct in all our operations. This Code of Ethics outlines the principles and expectations that guide our behavior and interactions with customers, partners, employees, and stakeholders.

By adhering to this Code, we aim to foster trust, integrity, and accountability in everything we do. All employees, contractors, and partners associated with RIVOT are expected to uphold these standards. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action or termination of business relationships.


1. Integrity and Honesty

We conduct our business with unwavering integrity and honesty, ensuring transparency in all dealings with customers, suppliers, and stakeholders.

  • All communications, including marketing, sales, and customer interactions, must be truthful and not misleading.
  • We prohibit any form of bribery, corruption, or unethical practices in our operations or supply chain.
  • Employees and partners must avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts promptly to management.
  • All financial records, transactions, and reporting must be accurate, complete, and compliant with applicable laws and regulations.

2. Respect and Fairness

We treat all individuals with respect, fostering an inclusive and fair environment for employees, customers, and partners.

  • We are committed to providing equal opportunities and prohibiting discrimination based on race, gender, religion, age, or any other protected characteristic.
  • All employees and partners must maintain professional conduct, free from harassment, bullying, or any form of disrespectful behavior.
  • We ensure fair pricing, terms, and conditions for all customers, adhering to applicable consumer protection laws.
  • Customer complaints and concerns will be addressed promptly, fairly, and respectfully.

3. Sustainability and Responsibility

RIVOT is dedicated to sustainable practices and corporate social responsibility, ensuring our operations benefit the environment and the communities we serve.

  • We strive to minimize our environmental impact through responsible manufacturing, waste reduction, and energy-efficient practices.
  • Our products are designed with sustainability in mind, promoting eco-friendly transportation solutions.
  • We engage with local communities to support education, innovation, and economic development.
  • All suppliers and partners are expected to comply with environmental regulations and ethical labor practices.

4. Compliance with Laws

RIVOT is committed to full compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards in every region we operate.

  • All business activities must adhere to the Companies Act, 1956, and other relevant legal frameworks in India and beyond.
  • We ensure compliance with consumer protection, data privacy, and intellectual property laws.
  • Employees and partners must report any suspected violations of laws or this Code to management or through designated reporting channels.
  • RIVOT will cooperate fully with regulatory authorities and conduct regular audits to ensure compliance.

5. Accountability and Reporting

We maintain a culture of accountability, encouraging open communication and reporting of ethical concerns without fear of retaliation.

  • Employees and stakeholders are encouraged to report unethical behavior or violations of this Code through confidential channels provided by RIVOT.
  • All reports will be investigated promptly and fairly, with appropriate actions taken to address violations.
  • We provide regular training to employees on ethical conduct and compliance with this Code.
  • RIVOT leadership is responsible for enforcing this Code and leading by example in all business practices.
